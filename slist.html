    Foundation for excellence(FFE) scholarship:

  • Students must have passed their 12th exam or equivalent no earlier than 2016, with a minimum of 70% marks or above.
  • The student must have taken admission on the basis of ranked obtained in the state & national level test for engineering exams.
  • Students who have taken direct admission in the above mention professional courses are not eligible.
  • Students starting their studies in BE, Btech & MBBS in the academic year 2022 are only eligible.
  • The student's family income should not be more than 2,50,000 per annum.
  • Students taking lateral entry in BE/ Btech course after doing diploma courses are not eligible.

Baba Gurbachan Singh Scholarship Scheme

  • You must be a first-year regular student pursuing a diploma or degree program from a recognized university in India
  • The family annual income should not exceed more than 3.50 lakh.
  • Must have scored a minimum of 60 % marks in class 12th.
  • Must not have taken admission through management or any other direct quota
  • Should not fail in last qualifying exam or detain by the institute for indiscipline or equivalent.
  • Must not be getting any other scholarship or similar scholarship.

S.N. Bose Scholarship

  • Students pursuing a Bachelor's or master's degree program from a recognized institution of India can apply for this scheme.
  • The disciplines of pursuing UG and PG degree are Atmospheric and Earth Sciences, Chemical Sciences, Engineering Sciences, Mathematical and Computational Sciences, and Physical Sciences.
  • Candidates who are studying Ph.D. cannot apply.

Central Sector Scheme (CSS) Of Scholarships 2022

  • Central Govt Scholarship for college students have basic eligibility conditions which students have to majorly fulfill are as follows
  • An aspirant must pursue a regular graduate or post-graduate course or professional courses such as medical or engineering from a recognized college/ University.
  • A candidate must secure above 80% in the relevant stream in the 12th standard.
  • The family income of the applicant from all sources must not exceed 6 lacs per annum
  • Candidates receiving any other scholarship cannot apply.

Vidyasaarathi-SNL Bearings Scholarship (B.E/B. Tech)

  • Aspirants can ensure their eligibility for this scholarship scheme from here
  • To be eligible for this scheme, students have to secure admission into a full-time B.E./ B.Tech course after passing 12th.
  • Applicants must have a minimum of 50% marks in SSC and HSC respectively.
  • Students having a family annual income of fewer than 5 lacs are only eligible for this scheme.
  • The employees and the children of SNL Bearing Ltd. cannot apply for this scholarship.
